ABOUT NARUMON'S HOMESTAY

Narumon's Homestay have a perfect location - in the hub of the Paraparaumu community. Only five minutes walk to the rail and bus station and another two minutes walk to the Coastlands shopping mall. We have a walkway to the base of the statue where you can enjoy great views of the entire Kapiti Coast and out to Kapiti Island.

Enjoy our private park-like surrounds and the sound of birds singing in the background. Stroll through the garden or simply rest up on one of the numerous outdoor seats. Our garden and glasshouse grows all the herbs needed for our Thai dishes. Our modern character home will ensure that you stay is relaxing and comfortable.
